Research Interest

  • Immersed Boundary Method (IBM)


    Immersed Boundary Method, popularly known as IBM, is a fast growing research trend among CFD practitioners, who are seeking a simple and efficient way to handle complex geometries. Unlike conventional CFD techniques, in IBM body fitted grid is not necessary for flow calculation, rather information is interpolated to solid-liquid interface from nearby grid points.
